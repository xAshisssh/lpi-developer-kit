## Example 1: Tesla Digital Twin

**Challenge:**  
Managing real-time performance and predictive maintenance of vehicles.

**Approach:**  
Tesla uses digital twins of cars by collecting real-time sensor data and simulating vehicle behavior in the cloud.

**Outcome:**  
Improved vehicle performance, predictive maintenance, and remote diagnostics.

## Example 3: Singapore Smart City Digital Twin

**Challenge:**
